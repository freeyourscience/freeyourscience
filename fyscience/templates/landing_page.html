What we provide for authors

Free Your Science is out to show authors like you which of your paywalled publications you can re-publish open access today and how to do it.

Find your paywalled publications

Publishers can keep your publications behind paywalls. This limits your scientific reach to readers with paid subscriptions. We help you to find these publications.

Identify open access pathways

Many publishers allow open access re-publication of your work, free of charge, today. Using a publisher policy database, we find the best pathway for your work to become open access.

Learn to re-publish open access

Depending on the identified pathway you can deposit your manuscript in places like arXiv or PubMed. We provide a guide on re-publishing to help you understand this process.
